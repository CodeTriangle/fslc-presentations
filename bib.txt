* https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Init
    * A great jumping-off point for research.
    * Has a lot of very valuable links.
* https://arxiv.org/abs/0706.2748
    * Paper from 2007 on Unix init schemes.
    * Gets into very experimental territory.
    * Came before systemd's homogenizing.
* http://smarden.org/runit/
    * Official website for runit.
    * Very nicely organized.
    * Understand runit in a few hours.
* https://skarnet.org/software/s6/
    * Official website for s6.
    * Introduction to many ideas about init.
    * Deeper dive than this presentation.
* runit and OpenRC man pages
    * When in doubt, just read the manual.
